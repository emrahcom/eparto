Adding a contact into the meeting list

eparto.net

Open menu

Select meetings

Click meeting's members

Click add to add a member

Select contact and submit

The member candidate will be added to your list but her status will be pending since she doesn't accept your invite yet

The candidate will see your invitation in her list when she opens her meeting list

When she accepts the invitation...

The meeting will be added into her meeting list...

And you will see her as a member in your list
